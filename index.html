About This Project

This is my personal YouTube video organizer where I structure all my learning materials. While it's designed for my personal use, anyone who wants to learn from my curated content is welcome to explore and benefit from it.

Feel free to browse through the categories and learn along with me!

Learning Philosophy

Organized learning leads to better retention and understanding. By categorizing educational content, we create mental models that help connect new information with existing knowledge.
